Installation Instructions

  1. Click the "Download for Android" button above
  2. Allow downloads from unknown sources in your device settings if prompted
  3. Open the downloaded APK file
  4. Follow the installation prompts
  5. Grant necessary permissions for location and camera access
  6. You're ready to use MedAI Patient!

System Requirements

  • Android 7.0 (API level 24) or higher
  • At least 50MB of free storage space
  • Internet connection required
  • GPS/Location services enabled
  • Camera access for X-ray uploads

Important Safety Notice

This app is designed to assist with medical emergencies but should not replace professional medical judgment. In life-threatening situations, always call your local emergency services (911, 112, etc.) immediately.
