Installation Instructions

  1. Verify your EMT/Driver certification status
  2. Click the "Download for Android" button above
  3. Allow downloads from unknown sources in your device settings if prompted
  4. Open the downloaded APK file
  5. Follow the installation prompts
  6. Complete registration with your certification details
  7. Wait for admin approval before going live

System Requirements

  • Android 7.0 (API level 24) or higher
  • At least 100MB of free storage space
  • Stable internet connection required
  • GPS/Location services with high accuracy
  • Camera and microphone access
  • Notification permissions for emergency alerts
